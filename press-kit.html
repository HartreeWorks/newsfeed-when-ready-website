Overview

Inbox When Ready is a Chrome extension that hides your Gmail inbox by default. All other views remain accessible as normal, so you can search your archive or compose messages without getting distracted.

Inbox When Ready is available free on the Chrome Web Store.

Install on Google Chrome

New feature! Inbox Lockout

Got an inbox habit? Let's fix that. The new Inbox Lockout feature lets you lock yourself out of your inbox at times you specify.

Try scheduling inbox lockouts for mornings, evenings and weekends. Your life will be better.

The story

The extension is designed in accordance with BJ Fogg's behaviour model, which predicts that the best way to improve behaviour is to optimise triggers and ability. In our case, the desired behaviour is sustained focus when working with email. This is achieved by suppressing a trigger (the naked inbox) and reducing ability (by locking you out of your inbox at times you specify).

The extension is inspired by people like Cal Newport and Tristan Harris.
